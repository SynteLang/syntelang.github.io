also what’s been happening

There was a fake[dac~] event at Corsica last week, which was great fun. So many people I know were there, Corsica really feels like a family to me. I met nesso who came over from Berlin to play an absolutely storming set. I was performed as well which was fun, although I still find that my brain really breaks down in front of a crowd like that.
Still managed to do some stuff with intermittently ‘freezing’ beats which I’ll continue to explore. Not the same as gating, although can (and did, oops) result in periods of silence. The technique is to periodically set the bpm to zero using a sq. If this catches a note mid-envelope it will hold at that value until released to continue. I’m going to try quantising the effect in time, adding variations etc in future.

Have some more things to post and should have more time in Feb to get them out the door fingers crossed. Today I contributed to a pastagang post - the encouragement has really inspired me to write more, hence this post you are reading. Thanks Lu!
